Why mac-setup?
⚡️

One command

Paste and go — works on a brand-new Mac with nothing installed. Homebrew, git, everything is bootstrapped automatically.

☑️

Interactive checklist

All 16 tools are pre-selected. Confirm with Enter to install everything, or toggle what you don't need.

🔄

Safe to re-run

Already installed? Skipped. Outdated? Auto-upgraded. No duplicate downloads, no wasted bandwidth.

💻

Apple Silicon & Intel

Detects your architecture automatically. Works on M1/M2/M3/M4 and all Intel Macs.

✨

Beautiful terminal

Configures Oh My Zsh with Powerlevel10k, autosuggestions, syntax highlighting, and Nerd Font — zero config needed.

🔓

Open source

One script per tool. Easy to read, fork, and extend with your own tools.
