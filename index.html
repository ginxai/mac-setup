Why mac-setup

Built to just work.

⚡️

One command

Paste and go — works on a brand-new Mac with nothing installed. Homebrew, git, everything bootstrapped automatically.

☑️

Interactive checklist

All 16 tools pre-selected. Hit Enter to install everything, or toggle off what you don't need.

🔄

Safe to re-run

Already installed? Skipped. Outdated? Auto-upgraded. No duplicate downloads, no wasted bandwidth.

💻

Apple Silicon & Intel

Detects your architecture automatically. Works on M1/M2/M3/M4 and every Intel Mac.

✨

Beautiful terminal

Configures Oh My Zsh with Powerlevel10k, autosuggestions, syntax highlighting, and a Nerd Font — zero config.

🔓

Open source

One readable script per tool. Easy to fork and extend with your own.
